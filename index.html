About


I am a 2nd year PhD student at LIMOS Research Lab/Mines St-Etienne, and I am grateful to be supervised by Xavier Delorme and Paolo Gianessi. We are interested in Exact Methods for Energy-Efficient Optimization in Production Systems.


I hold an MSc in Aeronautical Engineering and began my career as a scientific software engineer before pursuing a PhD to focus on research.


Interests


My research interests revolve around mixed-integer programming, particularly decomposition methods. Application areas include scheduling and other combinatorial problems in production systems.


I also have a general interest in graph theory and the integration of ML in mathematical programming.
