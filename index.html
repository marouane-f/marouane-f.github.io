About


I am a 3rd year PhD student at LIMOS Research Lab/Mines St-Etienne, and I am grateful to be supervised by Xavier Delorme and Paolo Gianessi. We are interested in Exact Methods for Energy-Efficient Optimization in Production Systems.


My research interests include combinatorial optimization and mixed-integer programming, especially decomposition methods. I'm also broadly interested in graph theory and, more recently, formal proof verification.
